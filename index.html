Hi, I'm Prarthana Mahipala.

I am a Front-End Engineer and UI/UX Developer with over 8 years of experience creating responsive, user-friendly web and mobile interfaces. I combine creativity with technical expertise to build engaging digital experiences.

I graduated from the University of Colombo Faculty of Science with a Bachelor of Science in Physical Science in 2006. After that, I earned a Master of Information Technology in 2011 from the University of Colombo School of Computing. I am now pursuing a Master of Business Management at the University of Colombo’s Faculty of Graduate Studies to strengthen my strategic and leadership skills.

Over eight years, I worked with multidisciplinary teams to design and build responsive, accessible interfaces for desktop, web, and mobile applications. Leading wireframing, prototyping, and front‑end development efforts, I ensured every product not only looked polished but also delivered intuitive, cross‑browser user experiences.


Today, I continue to deepen my expertise in modern front‑end technologies while advancing my business management studies.
